About Petes Technologies

Pioneering Africa's digital transformation through innovative technology solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Peter Ejeh, Petes Technologies emerged from a vision to bridge the digital divide in Africa. We recognized that existing solutions were often adapted from Western markets without considering Africa's unique challenges, opportunities, and user behaviors.

Our journey began with extensive research across 15 African countries, understanding local financial patterns, connectivity challenges, and cultural nuances. This deep understanding forms the foundation of every product we build.
